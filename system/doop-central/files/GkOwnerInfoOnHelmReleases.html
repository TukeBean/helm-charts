This check finds Helm releases that do not define owner info.

Why is this a problem?
Owner info such as maintainer's name tells other operators who is responsible for a specific release.
How to fix?
Add owner-info chart as a dependency to your Helm chart and add the necessary configuration parameters to your chart's Values.yaml file (instructions). The owner-chart will then deploy a ConfigMap that has all the necessary owner info in the format that this policy expects.
